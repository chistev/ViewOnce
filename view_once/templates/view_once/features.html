Features

Explore the robust features of ViewOnce designed to ensure your sensitive information remains private and secure.

Core Features

One-Time Access Only

Links can only be opened once before self-destructing.

End-to-End Encryption

Files and messages are encrypted in transit and at rest.

Temporary Hosting

Messages/files are automatically deleted after use or timeout.

Advanced Privacy Options

Set Expiration Times

Choose from various expiration times, such as 10 minutes, 1 hour, or 1 day, to control how long your content remains accessible.

Self-Destruct Options

Configure links to destroy themselves after being viewed, after a specific time, or based on a custom trigger.

API Access for Developers

Integrate ViewOnce into your own applications using our secure API for automated secure sharing.
