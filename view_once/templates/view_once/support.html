Frequently Asked Questions

How do one-time links work?

ViewOnce's one-time links ensure that your message or file can only be accessed once. After the recipient views the content, the link automatically expires and the content is permanently deleted from our servers, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Can I retrieve a deleted message?

No, once a message is deleted, it cannot be retrieved permanently from our servers, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How secure is my data with ViewOnce?

ViewOnce is committed to providing a secure and private platform for sharing sensitive information. We use industry-leading encryption and security practices to protect your data.
